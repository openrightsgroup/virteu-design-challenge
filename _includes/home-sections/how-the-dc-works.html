How the Design Challenge Works

You have until May___ to come up with a concept in response to the Design Challenge and present it in a clear and concise manner. We will consider all of the concepts submitted within the deadline and evaluate them.

On June 3rd, we will select the applicants who will proceed to the next round of concept development and will release a custom tool to support the last stage of concept development. The participants in this second round will have until June 20 to complete the second phase of concept development.

On June 24th, we will announce the finalists who will be invited to the presentation event as part of ORGCon.

ORGCon is Open Rights Group's conference in London. It's being held on Saturday 13 July 2019.

The selected concepts will be the ones that best answer the Challenge question and have the most potential for impact in the world, selected by our team according to the evaluation criteria listed below.
